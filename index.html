Hi, I'm Sharon!

Whether it’s crafting intuitive web interfaces, building scalable systems, or experimenting with AI-driven automation, I’m always driven by curiosity and a desire for continuous learning. I look forward to bringing these interests together in future projects, bridging the gap between web development and AI.
