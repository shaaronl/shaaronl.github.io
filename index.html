Hi, I'm Sharon!

I’m a third year computer science student at Cal Poly SLO and I’m passionate about building cool web applications and exploring the potential of AI, with a vision to combine the two in exciting ways. My journey as a developer has led me to work on various projects, including a merch-portal where I implemented a dynamic shopping experience, and a music review platform designed to provide users with a seamless way to rate and discover new music.

Beyond web development, I’ve also had hands-on experience with AI. Over the summer, I worked with the Jetson Nano fine-tuning the Resnet-18 model to be able to properly detect a rubber duck and dropping it into a bin. This project gave me a deeper understanding of computer vision and robotics, fueling my curiosity about AI’s real-world applications.

Whether it’s crafting intuitive web interfaces or experimenting with AI-driven automation, I’m always driven by curiosity and a desire for continuous learning. I look forward to bringing these interests together in future projects, bridging the gap between web development and AI.

On this site, you'll find my latest work, including the applications and web projects I’ve been creating. Let’s connect and explore new possibilities together!
